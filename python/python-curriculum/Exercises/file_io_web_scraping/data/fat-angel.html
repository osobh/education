        I love, love, love Fat Angel. It is a hidden (or not so hidden) gem of San Francisco. It is right off of Fillmore, it is a great spot if you are in the neighborhood. I've been going for about four years, and every time I go I find another great menu item. Mind you the menu is only one page (we aren't talking Cheesecake Factory selections) so hard to believe that I haven't tried it all. But on my last visit I discovered the Chicken Pot Pie. Divine! It may have been my first time trying it, but it certainly won't be my last. It is an open bowl of Chicken, carrots, peas, potatoes and the perfect amount of gravy and spices with a nice layer of buttery, flakey crust on the bottom and backed to perfection on the top. Man, I want to go back today just to try it again. It is almost sweet (in a good way) so I do recommend sprinkling with salt and pepper.

        They always have a kick bootie beer selection. And definitely keep them on the radar during sf beer week. Every evening they have a different delicious offering. I sampled five great Paradox beers and on another night 5 Bruery beers poured from bottles. (They had fifteen hard to find beers to choose from, so I had to narrow it down to one sample of five.)

        Our fall back food options have always been the bread with flavored butters. The most recent flavors were Bernaise, Chive, Chili, Maple Bacon and Molasses. You can try one for $5.50, three for $15 or all five for I think $25. That is a lot of butter, but I did feel compelled to try them all once and take home the left overs. This last time the Bernaise butter was my favorite. Delish.

        We also love their flatbreads, toppings change seasonally. Most recently we tried the butternut squash and kale. Perfect!

        And the mac n cheese, is great. Has a tangy hardier taste with the perfect amount of sauce to crunch ratio.

        Sometimes the prices can get a little steep if you don't pay attention when ordering, you think it may be a quick bite of food and a beer from your local gastropub, and next thing you know your tab is $90. But you can't blame them for having too many awesome things to order!

        I would like to have cold ice water, luke warm water is the worst. And a few veggie sides and sweet options. But this place is a great go to spot.

      Specialties

      30 wines by the glass from around the globe, 6 California draft wines & 9 artisan draft beers, and a revolving, eclectic 100+ bottle beer menu.  Our food is American comfort with mac n' cheese, chicken pot pie, beef sliders, huge salads and flatbreads.  Our ever revolving meat, cheese, and butter boards are not to be missed.

      History

      Established in 2010.

      Fat Angel was born out of a passion for The Fillmore District. We are a casual wine bar and cafe that serves comforting food and drink to the community. We offer a handpicked, eclectic mix of wine and beer that spans the globe, focusing on regions and producers that offer exceptional value and quality. Our restaurant was built with reclaimed, repurposed, and recycled materials from around San Francisco. This creates an intimate, old world environment, perfect for hanging out with friends, drinking a glass of wine, and eating a nourishing meal. Fat Angel's mission is to become The Fillmore's benchmark in food, service, price, & atmosphere.
